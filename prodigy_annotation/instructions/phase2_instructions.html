Phase 2 Schema

A guide to schema definitions for Phase 2 labelling.

Person - Title

Common noun referring to an established professional title.

Examples:

"CEO", "Minister", "Prime Minister", "Minister of Defence"

Person - Role

Common noun referring to the role a person can have in a specific context and that captures a relevant relationship the person has either with another person, organisation or government.

Examples:

"partner", "cyclist", "child", "children", "migrant"

Person - Occupation

Common noun referring to a job or a profession.

Examples:

"nurse", "GP", "MPs", "HGV driver", "lawyer", "scientist", "(medical) doctor", "teacher"

Location - Common Noun

Common nouns referring general place that is not a GPE or proper noun of a place.

Examples:

"cafe", "airport", "border", "garage", "road"

Economy - Sector

The name of an economic sector, in either the primary, secondary or tertiary area.

Examples:

"construction", "social care", "hospitality", "farming"

Event - Life Event

A significant life event that a user may go through. It implies a change in the circumstances of the subject and have policy/admin implications (and so in the relationship between the subject and government). A move from one STATE to another STATE. Can be both a Verbal Phrase (adopting a child) as a Noun Phrase (child adoption). Get non-comprehensive list from gov.uk/browse pages.

Examples:

"having a baby", "moving to the UK", "start a business", "becoming unemployed", "becoming a foster parent", "getting married", "retirement"

Finance - Financial Transaction

Common noun. Financial term that refers to the object of a transaction.

Examples:

"fine", "lease", "mortgage loan", "benefits", "fee", "surcharge", "refund", "funding", "rebate", "salary", "contract", "procurament"

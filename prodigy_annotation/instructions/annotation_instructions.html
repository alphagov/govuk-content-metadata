Phase 2 Schema

A guide to schema definitions for Phase 2 labelling.

Person - Title

Common noun referring to an established professional title. If it precedes a proper noun, it is likely to be a title.

Examples:

"CEO", "Minister", "Prime Minister", "Minister of Defence", “Captain”, “Assistant”, “Dr”, “Mr”. See

Person - Role

Common noun refers to the role a person can have in a specific context and that captures a relevant relationship the person has either with another person, organisation or government.

Examples:

"partner", "cyclist", "child", "children", "migrant", “refugee”, “volunteer”, “guarantor”

Person - Occupation

Common noun referring to a job or a profession.

Examples:

"nurse", "GP", "MPs", "HGV driver", "lawyer", "scientist", "(medical) doctor", "teacher"

Location - Common Noun

Common nouns referring general place that is not a GPE or proper noun of a place.

Examples:

"cafe", "airport", "border", "garage", "road"

Economy - Sector

The name of an economic sector, in either the primary, secondary or tertiary area.

Examples:

"construction", "social care", "hospitality", "farming"
